Who We Are

NurtureFirst Teaching Support is an educational staffing agency dedicated to connecting schools with professional, vetted, and passionate teaching assistants. Our mission is simple to ensure that every school receives dependable support and every teaching assistant feels valued, trained, and empowered.

We understand the importance of a positive learning environment, and we work closely with schools to provide the right staff for the right roles.

  • Fully vetted and trained teaching assistants
  • Fast and reliable staffing solutions
  • Support for SEN, 1:1 roles, and general classroom assistance

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen learning environments by providing schools with dependable, compassionate, and skilled teaching assistants. We aim to support every child’s academic and emotional growth by ensuring classrooms have the right staff at the right time. Through professionalism, reliability, and care, we strive to make a meaningful difference in education every single day.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted teaching support agency, known for excellence, integrity, and positive impact. We envision an education system where schools never struggle to find reliable support and teaching assistants feel valued, empowered, and inspired to grow in their careers. We aim to set a new standard for quality, consistency, and human-centred support in education.

Our Commitment to Schools

We are dedicated to helping schools create stable, nurturing, and productive classrooms. NurtureFirst Teaching Support offers fast and flexible staffing solutions, ensuring you receive qualified assistants who match your school’s culture and needs. We work closely with leadership teams, SEN departments, and classroom teachers to provide the right support whether for same-day cover, long-term assistance, or specialised SEN roles.

We take pride in being dependable, communicative, and always ready to respond when you need us the most.

Our Commitment to Teaching Assistants

We believe that great teaching assistants deserve great support. At NurtureFirst, we offer a welcoming environment where staff feel respected, trained, and appreciated. We provide flexible shifts, fair pay, and opportunities for personal and professional development. Our platform makes it easy to manage shifts, track your work, and communicate with our coordination team.

We are committed to helping you grow, feel confident in your role, and make a lasting impact in the classroom.
